PROJECTS
Project Name: Snake Bite Detection

Description 🐍 Snake Bite Detection: A Lifesaving Initiative 🩺

The very name "Snake Bite Detection" might seem intimidating, but rest assured, this project is all about saving lives using advanced image processing techniques. Our goal is to identify snake species from bite images swiftly, enabling timely and accurate medical treatment, especially for people in remote areas like deltas and forests.
The inspiration for this project struck me when I read a heartbreaking news story about a young girl, only 8 or 9 years old, who succumbed to a snake bite due to the unavailability of proper treatment. This tragic incident sparked a question in my mind: How can we prevent such unnecessary loss of life? The answer lay in technology.
Embarking on this journey, I began with an extensive case study. I met snake rescuers in Cuddalore district, lab technicians who handle venomous and non-venomous specimens, and doctors experienced in treating snake bite victims. Conversations with survivors provided invaluable insights. An exemplary fire officer in Palayamkottai and visits to snake farms and sanctuaries deepened my understanding of these reptiles.
Books were another crucial resource. 📚 We accessed rare reference materials, such as "Visam Kadi Thadupu Matrum Mudal Udavi Pairchi Kai Edu," thanks to higher officials who granted us special permission. These books are not available for sale and cannot be sourced easily, underscoring their value in our research.
The culmination of this extensive research and fieldwork is a project that has garnered significant recognition and numerous awards 🏆. But beyond accolades, our primary mission remains clear: to save snakes and save humans.
Join us in this vital endeavor to make a difference in the lives of those at risk from snake bites. Together, we can turn technology into a powerful tool for saving lives. Let's protect both humans and these fascinating reptiles. 🐍🌍
